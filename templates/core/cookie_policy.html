Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how ModernBlog uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. Understanding how we use cookies helps you make informed choices about your privacy.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit ModernBlog, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to website owners.

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a set period or until deleted

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled in our systems.

Authentication: Keep you logged in
Security: Prevent fraud and protect your account
CSRF Protection: Secure form submissions

Preference Cookies (Optional)

These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a personalized experience.

Theme Preference: Dark/light mode selection
Language: Your preferred language
Layout Settings: Content display preferences

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Google Analytics: Website traffic and user behavior
Performance Monitoring: Page load times and errors
User Engagement: Most popular content and features

Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements.

Ad Targeting: Show relevant advertisements
Social Media: Share buttons and social plugins
Retargeting: Display ads on other websites
