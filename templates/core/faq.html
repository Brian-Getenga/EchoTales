Questions

Got questions? We've got clear, honest answers.

Is ModernBlog free to use?
Yes! Reading, writing, and publishing on ModernBlog is completely free. No paywalls, no subscriptions required.
Can I write and publish articles?
Absolutely! Anyone can sign up and publish articles instantly. No approval needed.
Do you accept guest posts?
We don’t accept traditional guest posts. Instead, everyone publishes directly under their own name and profile.
Can I monetize my content?
Yes! We’re rolling out creator monetization tools (tips, subscriptions, etc.) in 2025.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action is permanent.
Who owns my content?
You do! You retain full ownership and rights to everything you publish.
